About Gratitude

A legacy of educational excellence since 2006

Our Story

Located in the heart of Mile 3, Nkwen, Gratitude Bilingual Nursery & Primary School was created in September 2006 by our late founder, Mrs. Mani Margarat Kuo. What began as a vision to provide quality, affordable education has grown into a thriving institution that serves as a true home away from home for children aged 3–11.

Our Mission

Gratitude is a hub of educational innovation with its primary purpose to provide affordable quality education in a religious environment, as its contribution to the development of our children. We combine modern learning techniques with conventional teaching methods to build children who are mentally, physically and morally sound.

Our Approach

We strive to give our students the best with very high disciplinary standards. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure and modern facilities provide a conducive arena of learning. This is why Gratitude is not just a place to study — it is every child's "home away from home."

Academic Excellence

Gratitude prepares children for the Common Entrance and First School Leaving Certificate Examinations (FSLC), setting them up for success in secondary school and beyond. We offer pre-care (kindergarten) and full primary school education for children aged 3–11.

Our Programs

Comprehensive bilingual education from ages 3 to 11

Nursery (Ages 3–5)

Play-based learning building foundational literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a caring, bilingual environment.

  • Bilingual instruction (English & French)
  • Foundational numeracy & literacy
  • Creative play & arts
  • Social and emotional development

Primary (Ages 6–11)

Full primary education preparing students for national examinations and a strong secondary school foundation.

  • Common Entrance Examination preparation
  • First School Leaving Certificate (FSLC)
  • Modern & traditional teaching methods
  • Character & moral development

Age Requirements

  • Nursery: Ages 3–5
  • Primary: Ages 6–11

Application Process

  1. Contact us to schedule a school visit
  2. Complete the application form
  3. Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous school records)
  4. Meet with our admissions team
  5. Receive your enrollment confirmation

  • Affordable quality education
  • Bilingual instruction (English & French)
  • Religious education and moral development
  • Modern facilities and teaching methods
